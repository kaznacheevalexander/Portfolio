about us

Our objective is to generate positive risk-adjusted returns for its capital partners via the investment in property development projects and established property assets.

We believe our strengths lie in an established track record, the depth of its industry relationships and its collaborative approach to development. These strengths allow Henshall to connect quality international, national and local tenants with tailored development and asset solutions, first-class development and asset management teams, and patient sources of capital (institutional investors and high net worth individuals).

Henshall’s principals have expertise in managing and developing properties in the hotel (accommodation), retail, industrial, office, aged care and residential sectors, and Henshall is currently seeking development and established asset opportunities in each of these sectors.

People

Grahame Chevalley
CHAIRMAN

Grahame is a founding principal of
Henshall.

Grahame co-founded GWH Build in 1995. Grahame has over 30 years of experience in the building and construction industry. Having held various positions in the construction industry including Site Supervisor, Project Manager, Construction Manager and Managing Director, Grahame brings a variety of construction industry expertise from both a contractors’ and owners’ perspective enabling him to provide unsurpassed service and deliver exceptional solutions to his clients.

Grahame is a passionate supporter and contributor of his community. He is heavily involved in volunteering for the NSW Rural Fire Service and a number of other community organisations, and is committed to his companies being good corporate citizens.

Jonathan Craig
Director – Development

Jonathan is a founding principal of
Henshall.

Jonathan is responsible for maintaining Henshall’s relationships with key industry participants and for identifying and executing development, redevelopment and existing asset opportunities.

Jonathan is a co-owner of GWH Build and has been Managing Director of that company since 2003. Jonathan first joined GWH Build in 1997 and has 20 years of experience in property development and construction. He holds a Bachelor of Construction Management (Honours) from the University of Newcastle.

BLAKE ALMOND
Director – Funds Management

Blake is a founding principal of
Henshall.

Blake is responsible for identifying fund opportunities, executing development and asset transactions, managing established property funds and maintaining relationships with capital partners, banks and other fund stakeholders.

Blake was previously a Vice President at Macquarie Capital, Macquarie Group’s investment banking division. During his time at Macquarie, Blake acted as a financial adviser to international and national companies on merger and acquisition and equity capital markets transactions in the mining and general industrial sectors.

Blake has a Bachelor of Commerce (Honours) from the University of Sydney and a Masters of Finance from INSEAD.
